About me

I'm from Aichi and live in Tokyo. I studied topology and 3DCG animation in college.
In 2015, as a web designer and front-end developer in a business company, I was mainly involved in the new development of owned media. After that, I got a degree in art while working, and in 2019 I changed my job to an e-commerce site management company for contemporary art. Therefore, after engaging in planning and management of art work exhibitions and planning and editing of web contents, I became a freelancer in 2021.
